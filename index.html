Key Insights
🔴 Sales Representatives have the highest attrition rate at 39.3% — nearly 4 in 10 leave. Requires urgent retention review.
⚠ Employees working OverTime attrite at 30.6% vs only 10.4% for non-overtime staff. Work-life balance intervention needed.
📊 Upto 5k salary slab accounts for 68.5% of workforce but carries 21.6% attrition — income is a significant flight risk factor.
🟢 Employees aged 36–45 are most stable with only 9.1% attrition. Younger workforce (18–25) is most volatile at 35.8%.
